About Me

Vishesh Javangula is a technical sales specialist at IBM. He will be joining either Ed Wright’s team, who specialize in Cognos, or Jon Levy’s team, who specializes in Data Analytics. Vishesh’s interest are in data analytics, AI, and cybersecurity.

Vishesh’s previous work was centered around blockchain and cybersecurity. He worked as a research assistant at George Washington University where he helped develop a model for how to supervise automated transactions across IoT devices while maintain data privacy. He also helped co-write the paper and assisted in analyzing current literature on the topic. Additionally, VIshesh executed computational chemistry simulations where he analyzed the effectiveness of water purifiers. Vishesh attended the George Washington University where he studied Biology and minored in Computer Science. He actively engaged in extracurriculars outside of the classroom and lead a team of volunteers to Kampala, Uganda where his team assisted in the funding and growth the NGO Set Her Free.

In his free time, Vishesh takes up photography, boxing, and lifting.
